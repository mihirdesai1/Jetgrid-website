Jet Card

The smarter way to fly private.

The JetGrid Jet Card is designed for those who value the flexibility of private aviation with the certainty of a fixed rate. Select your preferred aircraft category, purchase your hours, and fly when you need to — with an aircraft always available for you.

Our commitment includes 25, 50, or 100 flying hours per year across your chosen category. Unused hours carry forward, so you fly on your schedule — not ours.

  • Your rate is fixed for the life of your card

  • A dedicated concierge assigned to your account
